What is PregNut?

Nutrition guidance that respects your time.

PregNut is a food lookup tool for pregnancy nutrition. It helps you discover which foods deliver more (or less) of the essential nutrients for a healthy pregnancy.

Finding detailed nutrient data can be frustrating. PregNut organizes the evidence so you can see the best options fast, then dig deeper when you want to.

How to use PregNut

Two ways to explore.

Browse the best foods for a nutrient, or search a food and see the full profile (always in 100g portions).

01

Top Foods

The Top Foods view shows the most nutrient-dense options for each essential nutrient, broken down by food group.

Toggle between nutrients, then filter sources from processed (fortified) to natural (whole-food) sources.

Use the top search bar to open a full nutrient profile for any food.

The recommendations

Daily nutrient needs and benefits.

Needs come from the NIH. Benefits are summarized from trusted health sources.

Need

Calcium

1000mg

Baby benefit

Builds bones and teeth. Your growing baby takes the calcium she needs from your body, so to keep your bones and teeth healthy, you need to get enough to replace that amount. (1)

Mother benefit

Protects your bone density. It may also help prevent high blood pressure while you're pregnant. (1)

Need

Choline

450mg

Baby benefit

Helps prevent problems in the spinal cord and brain, called neural tube defects, and enhances brain development. (1)

Mother benefit

Builds strong bones and may help prevent high blood pressure. (1)

Need

DHA

0.3g

Baby benefit

Helps boost brain development, including communication (3) and vision. (4)

Mother benefit

May reduce your risk of heart disease in the future (1) and post-partum depression. (2)

Need

Folate (Folic Acid)

600mcg

Baby benefit

Helps protect against spinal cord birth defects during the first 30 days of pregnancy (1), in addition to other birth defects, miscarriage, and premature delivery. (5)

Mother benefit

Prevents anemia. (1)

Need

Iodine

250mcg

Baby benefit

Needed for brain and nervous system development; important for preventing stunted growth, severe mental disability, and deafness; important in preventing miscarriage and stillbirth. (1)

Mother benefit

Important for a healthy thyroid. (1)

Need

Iron

27mg

Baby benefit

Helps prevent premature delivery (1), low birth weight, and post-partum hemorrhages, and possibly cardiovascular risk for the baby in the future. (4)

Mother benefit

Wards off anemia in pregnant women. (1)

Need

Potassium

4700mg

Baby benefit

Too much potassium can adversely affect your child. (6)

Mother benefit

A small amount is necessary to keep blood pressure in check and maintain proper fluid balance; its essential for normal heart beat and energy. (1)

Need

Protein

70g

Baby benefit

Protein is crucial for your baby's growth throughout pregnancy. (1)
